MHA Anime Quiz!!!

How to Play:

  • Press Start Button to Begin
  • Answer as many question as you can in the given time (60 seconds).
  • Every question will contain four possible answers.
  • For every incorrect answer, 10 seconds will be deducted from your timer.
  • Your score will be calculated based on the remaining time you have left.
  • If you run out of time before answering all the questions, you lose.
  • Press Scoreboard Button to check Highest Scores.
